FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about my services, pricing, and process.

How much does a portfolio website cost in India?

A portfolio website costs between ₹999 and ₹4,999 depending on the package. The Basic plan at ₹999 includes a one-page portfolio with responsive design, contact form, free hosting, and SEO basics. The Premium plan at ₹4,999 includes a custom domain, admin panel, blog, analytics, and one month of support.

Do you provide SEO with every website?

Yes, every portfolio website package includes SEO optimization. The Basic plan covers SEO fundamentals like meta tags, mobile responsiveness, and page speed. The Professional and Premium plans include full SEO with keyword optimization, structured data, sitemap, and Google Search Console setup.

Can you help with college project development?

Absolutely. I offer full-stack college project development ranging from ₹3,000 to ₹8,000 depending on complexity. This includes database integration, documentation, reports, and deployment support. Mini projects are available from ₹1,000 to ₹3,000.

What is included in the resume writing service?

The resume writing service starts at ₹299 for a basic ATS-friendly resume in PDF format. The Professional plan at ₹599 includes a custom design, cover letter, and multiple formats (PDF + DOCX). The Executive plan at ₹999 adds LinkedIn optimization and unlimited revisions.

How long does it take to build a portfolio website?

A Basic portfolio website is typically delivered within 2–3 business days. Professional websites take 4–5 days, and Premium websites with admin panels and blogs take 7–10 business days. Timelines may vary based on content availability and revisions.

Do you offer ongoing support after delivery?

The Premium portfolio plan includes one month of free support after delivery, covering bug fixes, minor updates, and technical assistance. For other plans, ongoing support and maintenance can be arranged separately at an affordable hourly rate of ₹300/hour.
